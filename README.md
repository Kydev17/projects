## 🎮 Game Rules

In this game, you will attempt to guess a randomly generated number within a certain range. Here are the basic rules:

1. The game will ask you to guess a number.
2. You will receive hints whether your guess is too high or too low.
3. Keep guessing until you find the correct number.

Enjoy sharpening your guessing skills!
